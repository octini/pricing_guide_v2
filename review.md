The pricing guide looks solid, but here are some suggestions:
1. "Mantle of Spell Resistance" feels a bit underpriced. It gives advantage on all saving throws against spells, which is very powerful for an uncommon item. Consider bumping it or reviewing the fixed value.
2. The "Ring of Three Wishes" price is astronomical (like 400k+). While Wish is powerful, maybe cap it so it isn't literally priceless? Most guides cap single-use/limited-use legendary items around 100k-150k.
3. For "Broom of Flying", it's uncommon but provides unlimited, concentration-free flight. This is notoriously under-rarity in 5e. The price should probably reflect at least a Rare value, regardless of its official Uncommon rarity.
